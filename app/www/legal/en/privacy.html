Lumina Reader Privacy Policy

Version: v1.0 | Effective Date: January 2026

Core Principles

Lumina Reader highly values user privacy protection. This Software adopts a pure local architecture where the vast majority of user data is stored only on your device locally and not uploaded to any server.

Information We Collect

Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Imported book documents
  • Reading progress, bookmarks, annotations
  • Custom font files
  • Application setting preferences

Third-Party Services

The following features may involve interaction with third-party services:

  • **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Provided by Microsoft Azure, only transmits current paragraph text for voice synthesis

Information We Explicitly Do NOT Collect

  • Identity information such as real name, phone number, or email
  • Geographic location information
  • Contacts, photos, or videos
  • Data collected through cookies or tracking technologies

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally, not uploaded to the cloud
  • Application sandbox mechanism prevents other apps from accessing data
  • Users can export or delete data at any time

Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact us through the in-app feedback channel.

Our promise: Your reading belongs only to you.
